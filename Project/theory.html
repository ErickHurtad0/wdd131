Understanding Blues Music Theory

Discover the patterns, chords, and notes that define the soul of the blues.

The 12-Bar Blues Structure

The 12-bar blues is the foundation of most blues songs, following a specific chord progression pattern repeated over 12 measures.

    I  | I  | I  | I
    IV | IV | I  | I
    V  | IV | I  | V

Chord Progressions

Blues chord progressions create tension and resolution. Common chords include I, IV, and V in any key.

  • Key of C: C7, F7, G7
  • Key of G: G7, C7, D7
  • Key of A: A7, D7, E7
